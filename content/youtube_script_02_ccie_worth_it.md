## WHEN CCIE ISN'T WORTH IT (8:00-9:30)

**Be Honest With Yourself:**

**DON'T pursue CCIE if:**
- You're just starting out (get CCNA/CCNP first)
- You hate networking (seriously, pick something else)
- You're only doing it for money (burnout incoming)
- You think it's a shortcut (it's the LONG route)

**DO pursue CCIE if:**
- You love solving complex problems
- You want to architect enterprise networks
- You're committed to 6-12 months of hard work
- You already have hands-on experience

**Alternative Paths:**
- Cloud certifications (AWS Solutions Architect, Azure Expert)
- Security certifications (CISSP, OSCP)
- Automation/DevOps (Ansible, Python, Kubernetes)

"Those are ALL valuable. CCIE isn't the only path. But it's a damn good one if networking is your thing."
