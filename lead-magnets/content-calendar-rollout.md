## Risk Mitigation

**What if conversion rate is low (<15%)?**
- A/B test headline and CTA
- Reduce form fields (email only)
- Add social proof/testimonials
- Show preview of PDF content

**What if leads are low quality?**
- Add company size qualifier
- Include job title in form
- Adjust targeting on paid ads
- Refine content to attract right audience

**What if no one is booking consultations?**
- Reduce friction (remove calendar, just email)
- Offer shorter consultation (15 min instead of 30)
- Add consultation testimonials
- Make the value clearer in CTA
